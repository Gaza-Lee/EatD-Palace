About Us

We are passionate about crafting unforgettable dining experiences with locally sourced ingredients, bold global flavors, and heartfelt hospitality. Whether you're joining us for a cozy dinner, a weekend brunch, or a celebratory gathering, our menu, curated by award-winning chefs, is designed to delight both adventurous food lovers and those seeking comfort classics.

Our History

EatD Palace began as a simple dream shared over a kitchen table. We wanted to create a place where food tells a story, community gathers naturally, and every guest leaves feeling nourished in body and spirit. Founded in 1995 by Von Hassel, our journey started with a love for authentic flavors, time-honored recipes, and the belief that dining should be more than just a meal. It should be an experience. What began as a modest neighborhood eatery has grown into a beloved local destination, still guided by the same values: quality, care, and connection. Over the years, we've stayed true to our roots while evolving with the seasons and the tastes of our guests. Our chefs continue to draw inspiration from global traditions and local harvests, crafting dishes that honor the past while embracing the present. Thank you for being part of our story. We're honored to share our table with you today, tomorrow, and for many meals to come.
